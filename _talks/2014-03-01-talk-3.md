At KU Center for Genomics Research Symposium 2026, I presented the flash talk of my current research on explanations for telomere length variation across different orders in the plant kingdom. Some potential hypotheses we had are genome size, hybridization, and lifespan. Overall, we hope to discover more explanations and put that into a bigger picture.
